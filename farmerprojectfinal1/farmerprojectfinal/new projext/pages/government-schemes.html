Comprehensive Guide to Agricultural Government Schemes

About Government Schemes

The Government of India has launched numerous schemes to support farmers and promote agricultural development. These schemes provide financial assistance, subsidies, insurance, and other benefits to help farmers improve their productivity and income.

Below you'll find a comprehensive list of government schemes categorized by their purpose. Click on any scheme to learn more and access the official application portal.

💰 Direct Income Support Schemes
